PaperReading CUT&TAG

Posted on 2019-05-09 | Edited on 2019-06-03 | Comments: | Views:

Kaya-Okur H S, Wu S J, Codomo C A, et al. CUT&Tag for efficient epigenomic profiling of small samples and single cells[J]. bioRxiv, 2019: 568915.

主要结论

Cleavage Under Targets and Tagmentation (CUT&Tag)， a chromatin protein is bound in situ by a specific antibody, which then tethers a protein A-Tn5 transposase fusion protein. All steps from live cells to sequencing-ready libraries can be performed in a single tube on the benchtop or a microwell in a high-throughput pipeline, and the entire procedure can be performed in one day. We demonstrate the utility of CUT&Tag by profiling histone modifications, RNA Polymerase II and transcription factors on low cell numbers and single cells..
基本就三点：1）路子简单；2）操作简单，可批量，可一天就搞定；3） Histone modifications, RNA polymerase II 以及TF都可以，单细胞也能上。
So，缺点是什么呢？对于Ezh2这种Tn5也会引入open region的bias？如何消除?

背景

ChIP-seq以及之前变种的主要问题：1）由交联步骤导致的，低信号，高背景，epitope masking （这是个啥鬼？）； 2） 低得率，因而要求细胞比较多。 可能解决方法是啥？

ChIC-seq的主要策略流程： maps a chromatin protein by successive binding of a specific antibody, and then tethering a Protein A/Micrococcal Nuclease (pA-MNase) fusion protein in permeabilized cells without cross-linking. MNase is activated by addition of calcium, and fragments are released into the supernatant for extraction of DNA, library preparation and paired-end sequencing.

是所有的蛋白/抗体都能tethering the pA-MNase? 怎么tethering的？ 小伙伴的解答：可以用Protein A tethering, Protein A可以结合可能所有antibody, 没有结合特异性。 antibody/蛋白越大可能月没法顺利构建这个东西。小伙伴也说了，自己实验室的ChIC-seq是带cross-linking的，可能比不带的好

ChIC-seq的主要问题： it must be followed by DNA end polishing and adapter ligation to prepare sequencing libraries, which increases the time, cost and effort of the overall procedure. Moreover, the release of MNase-cleaved fragments into the superna- tant with CUT&RUN is not well-suited for application to single cell platforms。

这里CUT&TAG的关键就是，把 MNase这个酶，换成hyperactivated Tn5. really…..

具体的实验流程，值得注意的是，他们开始搞上spike-in了, 以后莫非都搞spike-in做个内参？:
where permeabilized cells are first mixed with an antibody, and then immobilized on Concanavalin A-coated paramagnetic beads, allowing magnetic handling of the cells in all successive wash and reagent incubation steps. For standardization between experiments, we used the small amount of tracer genomic DNA derived from the E. coli during transposase protein production to normalize sample read counts in lieu of the heterologous spike-in DNA that is recommended for CUT&RUN.

具体结果

看看genome browser截图就能说清楚所有事了。 Histone modification的结果，显示的很不错。ChIC-seq的histone的结果应该也不错。

至于TF的结果，首先用NPAT这个经典TF，在chr1和chr6上，只有80个左右peaks的，来看看测的准不准。然后就看CTCF的了，CTCF的除了真实的峰，似乎还有一些由Tn5导致的峰，所以比较宽点？

接着人用了CUT&RUN的数据定义了CTCF true peaks，从CUT&TAG数据里，得到true vs false, 发现motif是显著差的，因而，其实简单点，就可以直接用motif去筛选那些peaks，把由Tn5得到的筛掉一大部分。 其实他们的数据也有IgG的CUT&RUN，直观感觉应该是CUT&RUN里面应该也是有这些Tn5带来的Peaks的，可以用来筛掉假的。问题来了，没有motifs的那些TF，咋从分析上去掉可能的假阳性？
