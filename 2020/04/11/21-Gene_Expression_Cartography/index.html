PaperReading Gene expression cartography

Posted on 2020-04-11 | Edited on 2020-04-25 | Comments: | Views:

Nitzan, M., Karaiskos, N., Friedman, N. et al. Gene expression cartography. Nature 576, 132–137 (2019). https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-019-1773-3

why read it

看这标题这么酷，就该读一读了。
搞scRNA-seq算法上顶刊也是值得跪着读的, computational biologist 去掉computational似乎指日可待。
软件： novosparc, example

所针对的问题

To solve: predicts locations of single cells in space by solely using single-cell RNA sequencing data.
就是根据表达无参考重构细胞位置，听上去以后拿到个tissue就是打打散一波测，然后就能拿到个virtual tissue了，挺酷的。

操作流程

Hypothesis: Although the number of ways to spatially arrange (or ‘map’) sequenced cells in tissue space is enormous, our hypothesis is that gene expression in the vast majority of these arrangements will not be as organized as in the real tissue. 听上去像是最低自由能假设？
实操: We formulate an interpolation between entropically regularized Gromov–Wasserstein and optimal-transportobjectives, which serves to satisfy the assumption of structural correspondence between gene expression space and physical space, and to match prior knowledge when available. 看到这种看不懂的Gromov-Wasserstein数学概念后， 决定试试软件就好了，不尝试自己写。
